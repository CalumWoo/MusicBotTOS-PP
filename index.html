Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026  ·  Questions? Contact us

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what data Music Bot ("the Bot") collects, how it is used, and how it is stored. We take your privacy seriously. The Bot collects only the minimum data required to function. We do not sell, share, or monetise any user data.

2. Data We Collect

When you interact with the Bot, the following data is stored in our database:

Data Why it's collected
Discord User ID To identify which items you own, your wishlist, and your roll history within each server
Discord Guild (Server) ID To scope your inventory, claims, trades, and roll limits to the correct server
Claimed items & pins To track your personal collection and pinned items per server
Wishlist entries To display your wishlist and flag items as claimed
Trade records To process and store pending and completed trades between users
Roll rate-limit data To enforce fair roll limits (15 rolls per 15-minute window)
Timestamps To record when items were claimed, trades were initiated, and roll windows started
We do not collect your Discord username, email address, profile picture, message content, or any personal information beyond your User ID and Guild ID.

3. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used exclusively to:

  • Run the Bot's core features (rolling, claiming, trading, wishlists)
  • Enforce per-server, per-user rate limits
  • Display your inventory, wishlist, and stats to you and other server members who request them
  • Resolve disputes or investigate abuse

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics sold to third parties, or any purpose beyond operating the Bot.

4. Data Visibility

Some data stored by the Bot is visible to other users by design:

  • Your inventory can be viewed by any server member using $inventory @you
  • Your wishlist can be viewed by any server member using $wishlist @you
  • Server roll stats are visible to everyone via $stats

This is intentional — the Bot is designed for community play. If you would prefer not to have your data visible this way, please remove yourself from Bot interaction by not using its commands.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as the Bot is active in servers you use. If you wish to have your data deleted:

  • You can remove items from your inventory using $divorce <name>
  • You can remove wishlist entries using $wishremove <name>
  • For a full data deletion request, contact us at harryclove0@Gmail.com with your Discord User ID

6. Third-Party Services

The Bot retrieves music metadata (artist names, album art, preview URLs, genre information) from the Deezer API and MusicBrainz. When you trigger a roll or lookup, a query is sent to these APIs. No personal information is sent — only search terms for music data. Please refer to Deezer's Privacy Policy and MetaBrainz's Privacy Policy for their data practices.

7. Data Security

Your data is stored in a SQLite database hosted on our server infrastructure. We take reasonable precautions to secure this data. However, no system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required to use Discord in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has used the Bot, please contact us so we can remove their data.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable law (such as GDPR or UK data protection law) to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at harryclove0@Gmail.com with your Discord User ID. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data requests, please contact us at harryclove0@Gmail.com or reach out through our Discord support server.
